Watermelon Salad with Feta

Description

This refreshing summer salad is a perfect balance of sweet, salty, and peppery flavors. Juicy chunks of ripe watermelon are paired with crisp arugula leaves and crumbled feta cheese, creating a vibrant medley of textures and tastes. A light drizzle of olive oil and a squeeze of fresh lime or balsamic glaze tie everything together, making it a simple yet elegant dish. Whether served as a starter or a side, this salad is a crowd-pleaser for warm-weather gatherings or a quick, healthy lunch.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • Â½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
  • 1 diced cucumber

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar and salt together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add tomatoes, arugula, red onion and cucumber; toss to coat.
  3. Gently stir watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
